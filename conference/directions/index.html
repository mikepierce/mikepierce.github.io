Directions

The conference will take place at the University of California, Riverside, on May 18. It will be held in the UC Riverside Math Department, located on the 2nd floor of Skye Hall (formerly Campus Surge). See below for a photo of the building. The Entrance is at the northeast corner of the building.

If you registered before May 10 and requested parking, there will be a parking permit available for you at the welcome kiosk on West Campus Drive. Just tell them your name. This permit will allow you to park in Lot 24 off of Canyon Crest Drive (P24 in the map below). If you registered after May 10, you can park in Lot 24 for a fee or park for free on Linden street between Rustin and Canyon Crest, or on Canyon crest between Blaine and Linden.

See the campus map for a detailed layout of the campus and surrounding area. And if you have any questions, direct them to Ethan Kowalenko at mathconn@math.ucr.edu.
